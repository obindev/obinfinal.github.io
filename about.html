About us

Elite Group is a project that start between friend on the Campus of Minnesota State University Mankato.
Obin Georges Koffer (CEO and founder), of that Digital content startup was always interested by the role of
in designing most of life most beautiful element. From cars which are more and more designing with atomic
bond of elegance to houses and plane. Computer and design are really the future of humanity.

With a small investment, Obin started Elite as brand name for his new music video company. Although, he struggle
because the music business here in the USA is competitive and the director is even worse. Nobody want to be left
a new camera upgrade, new color pattern, a range of quality. It is not cheap to go with the bus when you can't
even catch it or afford the ticket. That is when he introduced a total different path in the company: the graphic
designing and the development group. Here is how, Elite goes from Elite to group. Now the money is coming better
it was before. Elite Group CEO does not waste time hiring new models, new staff, and invest many thousands of dollars
on the best equipment they can afford.

Now business is flowing. The images got better. Obin team are very attentive to the single detail of the creation.
This give them a very good reputation and the start collaborating with big Label like Elevate your dream and Jean Kablan
The start working with Island africa, a branch of Universal Music Group (Africa). Helping with movie special effect and are
responsible for many good event in Minnesota, work on the music of many big minnesota rap/ rnb star and the commercial demand
is on the peach. Right now they are working on extending the startup to more African and European location, get to work with
as much businesses as they can. And Make Minnesota one big entertainment city. Help us we can do it together.
